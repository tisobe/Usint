The duty period is usually Monday through Sunday, but occaionally a period starts and/or end on different day. Periods which do not start on Monday and/or end on Sunday are marked by aqua.

If you like to scheulde your duty on the standard period, just select your name (or any other POC name) from "Contact" column of the period, and click "Update" buttom. Your poc id will appear on the column "Assigned By". You can submit more than one entries at a time.

If you like to schedule your duty in a none standard period, just change the dates accordingly. It will either adjust adjuascent period(s) or create another row to fill a gap. In the case of the overlapping, it will modify the overlapped period. If the adjustment is not possible (e.g. the affected row is already signed up), it will adjust in the range which does not affect the signed up period.

You can do the same for the already scheduled period. Just open the period by clicking "unlock" button, and take the same procedure described above. However, this works only when poc id in the "Assigned By" column is the same as yours ({{ submitter }}).

IMPORTANT: After click 'Update', or 'Unlock', please do not click any other buttons until the page is completely reloaded.
