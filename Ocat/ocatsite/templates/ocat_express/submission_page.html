Please type all obsids which you want to approve. The obsids can be delimited by any of:

  • "," (comma)
  • ";" (semi-colon)
  • "/" (right slant)
  • blank space(s)
  • tab(s)
  • "-" (dash) for a continuous case (the values are INCLUSIVE)

Examples

  • 12507, 12508, 12509 gives
    [12507, 12608, 12509]
  • 12507 12508; 12509/12510 gives
    [12507, 12508, 12509, 12510]
  • 12507-12510, 12512 gives
    [12507, 12508, 12509, 12510, 12512]
  • 12507-12510, 12512; 12514-12517 gives
    [12507, 12508, 12509, 12510, 12512, 12514, 12515, 12516, 12517]
